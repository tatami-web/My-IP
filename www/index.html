Usage Information

"My IP" is a lightweight Android application designed to quickly display your current IP address and its associated country flag.

Key Benefits

  • Does not require storage permissions.
  • No data is collected or stored.
  • Simple, intuitive interface.
  • Accurate IP address display.

Additional Features

  • It is ideal for users who want real-time information about their internet connection, whether they are using Wi-Fi, mobile networks, VPNs, or proxies.
